WHAT IS CHIP-IO?

ChIP-IO is a user-friendly web application designed to provide up-to-date, uniformly processed ChIP-Seq data and gene-specific transcription factor regulatory information.

OUR MOTIVATION

Chromatin immunoprecipitation followed by sequencing (ChIP-Seq) continues to be a critical informer of protein-DNA binding. As the amount of data continues to grow and the needs of researchers continues to become more complex, there is a need for up-to-date, user-friendly tools to serve these needs.

With the initiation of projects such as the Human Cell Atlas and the rise of techniques like single-cell RNA-seq,for example, it is clear that the field is moving towards more and more complex analysis at narrower and narrower scopes. ChIP-IO was designed with this in mind, allowing for tissue-specific and other metadata-specific analysis to meet increasingly nuanced needs.

Technical Details

ChIP-IOs underlying analysis process currently consists of two main steps. First is data collection, cleaning, and uniform processing for creation of the core database. Second is the custom-query pipeline for assessing transcription-factor gene regulatory targets. The former is external to this application, but can be used to update the reference datasets periodically. Current processed data can be directly downloaded under the "DOWNLOADS" tab. The latter is housed in this application under the "CONSTRUCT QUERY" tab. It handles user-provided queries and actively delivers the results. Details and schematics for both are provided below.
