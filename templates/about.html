OUR MOTIVATION

Chromatin immunoprecipitation followed by sequencing (ChIP-Seq) continues to be a critical informer of protein-DNA binding. ChIP-IO serves as a repository for the thousands of salvageable transcription factor ChIP-Seq datasets in existance, as well as a platform for delivering rapid custom analysis on this data.
